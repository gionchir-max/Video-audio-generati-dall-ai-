# Frida Kahlo — prompts clip meta.ai (39 clip da 5s)

**Stile globale**: HYPER-SATURATED Mexican folk colors maxed out — electric magenta, glowing cobalt blue, blazing marigold yellow, blood crimson red, vivid emerald green, photorealistic cinematic, Canon EOS R5, 9:16 vertical, shallow depth of field, film grain, no text, no captions, no watermarks

**Bibbia personaggio Frida (adulta, 22-47 anni)** — usare SEMPRE "the Mexican woman painter":
- Mexican woman, dark hair tightly braided with bright pink and yellow flowers and red ribbons woven in
- Thick connected dark eyebrows forming one bold line above the nose, faint dark moustache above the lip
- Intense dark almond eyes, olive skin, full lips often painted bright crimson
- Traditional Tehuana dress: vibrant embroidered blouse, long flowing magenta or crimson skirt, heavy gold layered necklaces, large dangling earrings

**Bibbia personaggio Diego (40+)** — usare SEMPRE "the large bald muralist":
- Mexican man, very large heavy build, round face, balding head with grey side hair, bulging frog-like eyes, thick lips
- Wears worn paint-splattered overalls or rumpled grey suit

**Bibbia personaggio Trotsky** — usare "the bearded European exile with round glasses":
- Older European man, white pointed goatee beard, round wire-rim glasses, swept-back grey hair, dark suit

**Setting**: Coyoacán Mexico, 1907-1954. Casa Azul (cobalt blue colonial house with magenta inner courtyard), Mexican folk environments.

## Note operative meta.ai

- **PRIMA di inviare**: cliccare bottone "Crea video" sulla home
- **Una chat ogni 3 clip** (per evitare freeze del bottone Invia)
- **Ogni prompt INIZIA con `5-second cinematic video clip:`** + `hyper-saturated colors`
- **Verbo movimento esplicito** in ogni prompt
- **Linguaggio neutro** — mai "Frida", mai "Diego", mai "Trotsky" — sempre "the Mexican woman painter" / "the large bald muralist" / "the bearded European exile with round glasses"
- **Salva i video** scaricati come `clips/01.mp4` … `clips/39.mp4`
- Se meta.ai risponde con "Oops" → skippa e continua
